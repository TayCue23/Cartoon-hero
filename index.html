About Michelangelo

First introduced in 1984 in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles issue no. 1 comic as one of 4 teen turtles with a passion for ninjutsu Michelangelo has become one the most beloved and recognized characters in western media. This mean green fighting machine has enjoyed decades gracing comic panels, TV & movie screens, and toy shelves all over the world. Over the years this fun loving reptile has gone through many iterations with changes to his character design and even his catchphrases, but at his core he has always remained Mikey.

Michelangelo's Characteristics

  1. His signature color is orange
  2. He is most often depicted weilding nunchucks
  3. He is often protrayed as the youngest of his brothers
  4. He is the fun loving party dude of the group
  5. He loves pizza and skateboarding
  6. His most commonly used nickname is Mikey
